About me

Hi! I’m Hanna Shibata, a Multidisciplinary Designer/Art Director with 10+ years of experience across continents and various fields.

After starting my career as a packaging designer in Tokyo, I expanded my role into branding design as an in-house creative, where I managed relations with internal and external stakeholders, building a creative team from scratch. To pursue my graduate studies at Pratt Institute, I left Tokyo and continued my career as an in-house Designer/Art Director in the United States since 2020.

I’m now taking time to expand my skill set and explore the digital realm of design.
